Who We Are

Solodarity Group is a humanitarian and development agency dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable communities across Jubbaland. We work hand‑in‑hand with local leaders, youth, women, and families to create sustainable solutions that strengthen resilience and build brighter futures.

Our programs focus on clean water access, education, health services, food security, and livelihood support — ensuring that every individual has the opportunity to thrive.
