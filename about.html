About Solodaritysom

Empowering communities across Jubbaland through sustainable development and humanitarian action.

Our Mission

To support vulnerable communities in Jubbaland by providing essential services, promoting resilience, and enabling long‑term development through inclusive, community‑driven initiatives.

Our Vision

A peaceful, resilient, and thriving Jubbaland where every individual has access to opportunity, dignity, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We stand with communities in need, offering support with empathy and respect.

Integrity

We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all our work.

Community

We believe in local leadership and community‑driven solutions.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to improve lives sustainably.
